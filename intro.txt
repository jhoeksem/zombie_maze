There exists a cursed forest, hidden deep in the valley between the Blottory Mountains. In the forest the only thing more certain than death is suffering. All those who die here are cursed, and have their souls roam the forest forever lamenting their regrets. Why, you may ask, would anyone ever set foot in this forest then? The answer is that in the center of the forest lies an Oasis where the Great Fairy lives. Any Traveler who makes it to her will receive one wish within her power. Many have come and many have fallen.
